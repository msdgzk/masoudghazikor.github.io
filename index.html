Welcome to my GitHub page!

I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Electrical Engineering & Computer Science (EECS) at the University of Kansas (KU). In 2020, I earned my BSc degree in telecommunication engineering with a minor in computer engineering from K. N. Toosi University of Technology (KNTU) in Iran. In my BSc project, I focused on the restoration methods in optical fiber networks. After finishing my BSc, I started my PhD studies at the University of Kansas in 2022. I joined the WCNS group to pursue my research interests in wireless communications, interference analysis, and UAV communication. Besides research, I enjoy playing billiards, volleyball, and video games, as well as listening to rap songs and rapping in my free time!
