Research

My current research is focused on developing robust and reliable wireless communications, mainly for the following areas:

  1. UAV Networks: Developing a theoretical framework to analyze the throughput and PSNR between UAV and ground/aerial nodes in unlicensed spectrum bands and optimizing the cross-layer parameters to maximize the average throughput and PSNR.
  2. mmWave Networks: Developing a simulation framework that accurately models various blockage scenarios for mmWave systems. The model captures all the characteristics of the blocker and the locations of the mmWave transmitter and receiver.
  3. Satellite Networks: Implementing a satellite communication model to analyze the outage probability between the geostationary earth orbit (GEO) satellite and ground users by serving different users in the beam.
  4. PFL Networks: Implementing a channel-aware neighbor selection policy for personalized federated learning (PFL) networks to choose the desired neighbors according to the transmission error.
